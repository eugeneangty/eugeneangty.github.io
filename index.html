Welcome to Eugene's homepage. I'm currently a Ph.D. student at NUS Institute of Operations Research and Analytics. I obtained my Bachelor of Science with Honours (Highest Distinction) in Applied Mathematics from the National University of Singapore.

My research interests focus on network analysis, graph theory and their applications in operations research. My advisors are Professor Andrew Lim, Assistant Professor Soh Yong Sheng and Adjunct Assistant Professor Prasanta Bhattacharya.

I have worked with the Manpower Research and Statistics Department, Ministry of Manpower through NUS Research in Industrial Projects for Students programme in 2020, on natural language programming text classifications.
