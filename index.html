Welcome to Eugene's homepage. I'm currently a Senior Data Scientist at Micron and an Adjunct Instructor at Department of Analytics and Operations, National University of Singapore (NUS). I obtained my Ph.D. at NUS Institute of Operations Research and Analytics and Bachelor of Science with Honours (Highest Distinction) in Applied Mathematics at NUS.

My research interests focus on network science, graph theory and their applications in operations research. My advisors are Professor Andrew Lim, Assistant Professor Soh Yong Sheng and Adjunct Assistant Professor Prasanta Bhattacharya.
